# Cybersecurity Governance, Law & Strategy Portfolio

This repository collects strategic and legal analysis projects developed during my Master's Degree in Cybersecurity at the **University of Pisa**. These works focus on the organizational, human, and regulatory dimensions of security.

---

## 🛡️ Project 1: Securing GlobalPharma
**Strategic Cybersecurity Culture & Organizational Plan**

A comprehensive 12-month roadmap designed to modernize the security posture of a global pharmaceutical company.
- **Key Topics:** CISO-CEO reporting lines, Security Champions programs, KPIs for phishing simulations, and ISO 27001 standards.
- **Team:** Matteo Bellini, Giorgio Cecchi, Luca Giannini, Andrea Luchetti, Jacopo Zecchi.

## ⚖️ Project 2: NIS2 vs. GDPR
**The Dual Notification Challenge & Regulatory Analysis**

A comparative study of European regulations, using the **2018 British Airways Data Breach** as a practical case study to analyze reporting overlaps.
- **Key Topics:** Incident reporting timelines (24h/72h), definitions of "Significant Incident", administrative fines, and **Supply Chain Security** accountability.
- **Team:** Matteo Bellini, Giorgio Cecchi, Luca Giannini.

## 🎯 Professional Value
While technical controls (like encryption and firewalls) are vital, these projects demonstrate my competence in **GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance)**. Understanding how to align cybersecurity with business goals and EU law is essential for modern security management.
